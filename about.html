Our Profile

Welcome to social Education Center, a premier vocational training college dedicated to empowering the youth with practical skills and knowledge for a successful career. With a strong focus on hands-on learning , We provide industry-relevant training programs designed to meet the demands of today's job market. Our experienced faculty, comprised of industry professionals, are commited to delivering high-quality education and metoring students to excel in there choosen vocations. From the state-of-the-art facilities to cmprehensive resources, we offer a conducive learnig environment that nurtures creativity ,innovation and profassional development. Join us at Social Education Center, A vocation Community College and embark on a transformative journey towards a rewarding and fulfilling career. Social Education Center is Catholic owned College, established in 1974 with registration number VTC/74/85 under Morogoro Diocese.

OBJECTIVE

  1. Equip students with practical skills and knowlege necessary to succeed in their chosen vocations.
  2. Foster a strong work ethic and professional attitude among students, preparing them for the demands of the workforce.
  3. Provide hands-on training opportunites that simulate real-world work environments, enabling students to apply their learing effectively.
  4. support students in developing interpreneurship and leadership skills, empowering them to start their own successful business or take on leadership roles in their chosen fields.

OUR MISSION

To provide quality vocation education that is equitability accessible to all, as emphasited in the medium-term development plan of Tanzania(vision 2025) and Milleniums Development goals.

OUR VISION

To implove access to high quality vocational education through training programs tailored to meet the need of the region and wider national community.
